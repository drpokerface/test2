Introducing Virtualgold


Quite potentially one of the most lucrative oppurtunities in the crypto space, virtualgold is the worlds first tokenless digital asset stored on the ethereum blockchain that uses an innovative model to sustainably increase its value. Unlike a standard token, virtualgold has no fixed total supply. The total amount of virtualgold in existance gets automatically updated as people buy and sell so as to prevent the price from falling. It has been created in such a way that its value steadily grows based upon demand, but at the same time even if the demand goes to zero holders get a certain level of protection. This is because, unlike other cryptocurrencies, virtualgold can be refunded to the smart contract intself. It is guarenteed by the smart contract that no investor can lose beyond 29% of their original investment in ETH.




Price Backed by Ethereum


The ethereum that is used for buying virtualgold is sent to a decentralized smart contract. Therefore for every microgram of virtualgold you own its equivalent value in ETH is always secured within this smart-contract. When a holder wants to convert his/her virtualgold back into ethereum, the corresponding value in ETH is sent to the holder's wallet from the smart-contract, and the holders virtualgold gets destroyed

How Virtualgold grows in value?


When someones convert ethereum to virtualgold, as mentioned above, the ethereum goes to the smart contract to back the price per gram of virtualgold, and the corresponding amount of virtualgold is created and given to the holder. But also there is a fee on every conversion. This fee gets added to the contract without generating any virtualgold. This therefore sustainably increases the price per gram of virtualgold after every purchase



Price Dump Resistant


Even if everyone sells, the price doesn't fall due to dynamic total supply technology. When a holder converts of virtualgold back into ethereum, their virtualgold gets destroyed. This therefore proportionately reduces the total grams of virtualgold in existence and thus maintains the price per gram ratio within the smart contract Assume the current price for 1gram of virtual gold is $100 dollars and you own 2grams. Even if everyone sells their holdings $200 will still be left within the contract exclusively for you. This value can never go down as people sell. But it would increase as more and more people buy.
